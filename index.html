🏛️ History Made on September 21st, 1983

📢 Anti-Marcos Demonstrations in Manila

Violent demonstrations erupted in Manila against President Ferdinand Marcos, resulting in the tragic death of 11 protesters - part of growing resistance against the authoritarian regime.

💻 Tech Innovation

The world was witnessing the early days of personal computing with IBM's PC XT making waves in the technology sector.

🎬 Entertainment Boom

Return of the Jedi was dominating box offices as the highest-grossing film of 1983, completing the original Star Wars trilogy.

🎵 Music Revolution

Michael Jackson's Thriller album was reshaping the music industry, with MTV changing how we consumed music forever.
